Teaching perspective

The intention of the New Mathematical School is simple: to give the student the minimum of instructions so that she is the one who finds the way. We do not want to be a traditional school where the student first listens, and then repeats specific exercises.

We want to do it in reverse:

We want to hear from the student first, what problem does she want to solve. Then we ask what ideas you have and from there we encourage you to explore those that we as teachers or guides we know they can fix the problem. The intention is that the student works and thinks for herself, and does not limit herself to copying and repeating mechanically as is currently the case in almost all classes. schools in Catalonia.
